ABOUT ME

Programmer, Photographer, Powerlifter

Born and raised in Brooklyn. Grew up washing dishes in the back of a restaurant and delivering sushi at 16. Spent four years in Ithaca, where I really learned to embrace the cows and the cold. I graduated with a degree from Cornell University, but more importantly, that's where I discovered my three passions.

Started programming in a required high school intro CS class in LISP and somehow wound up working across the nation in Wisconsin, Silicon Valley, and finally NYC for Google.

Started photography the day before a trip to Yosemite in an attempt to capture California's beauty. Ended up taking every shot in auto, which left me yearning to do better. Luckily, got the chance to really up my game during a post-grad trip to Asia which kept the camera glued to my hands everyday for 50 days. (I shoot with a Nikon D750 primarily with the 24-70mm)

Started powerlifting after dabbling in high school wrestling. My friend Joe taught me the importance of form, which really instilled the right mentality in me. Fast forward 5 years, my top lifts to date have been:

Squat: 385      Bench: 265      Deadlift: 405
